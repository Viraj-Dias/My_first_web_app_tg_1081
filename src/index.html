About Us

The University of Ruhuna, established in 1978, is the only university in the Southern Province of Sri Lanka. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties, fostering excellence in education, research, and community service.

Our Faculties

Faculty of Engineering

Located in Galle, offering degrees in Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical Engineering.

Faculty of Management and Finance

Focused on business, economics, and financial studies.

Faculty of Fisheries and Marine Sciences

Specializing in marine biology and aquaculture.
